Blue Barn's Thai Chicken Salad

sfweekly.com

THAI CHICKEN chili-lime mary’s chicken, carrot, snow peas,

cabbage, scallion, herb mix, almonds, sesame seeds, crispy wontons,

peanut-sambal dressing & house romaine mix

  • 4 cups chopped romaine
  • ½ cup shredded carrots
  • ½ cup julienned snow peas
  • ½ cup orange segments
  • 1 tablespoons sesame seeds a mix of black and white
  • 1 tablespoon chopped mint
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon chopped Italian parsley
  • cabbage
  • almonds/peanuts
  • crispy wontons
  • 2 tablespoons minced green scallions
  • ¾ cup roasted chicken, cut into ½-inch cubes
  • ¼ cup Thai peanut dressing
  • sambal olek
  • lime
  1. Toss all ingredients together in a large bowl. Add the dressing and toss thoroughly (it should just coat the ingredients)
